Please note: this patent term calculator is experimental and under heavy development. Not all relevant calculations have been implemented. For example, term disclaimer is not implemented, and neither is patent term extension for non-USPTO delays. About the only thing working is that the app fetches term adjustment from the USPTO and does a calculation.

Also, note that it only works for patents 5,000,000 and above. All patents earlier than that are expired anyway. And for now, it only accepts numbers without commas. There is a lot of development left to do, and I am only a beginner at this. Please be patient.

That said, try it out by typing in a patent number 5000000 or higher in the box and hit sumbit.
